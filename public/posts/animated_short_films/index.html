My Favorite Animated Short Films

I been watching these short animated videos on YouTube. Many of them are silent (no dialogue). They’re so well-made. Some made me cry. Some are more creepy, dystopian vibes with social commentary. It’s a whole movie experience in under 10 minutes.

Made me cry

  • One Small Step (appreciate your silent supporters) ***hella tears
  • Umbrella (have empathy for others)
  • Let’s Eat (Asian mother and daughter relationship)
  • Hair Love (family)
  • WiNDUP (hospitalization)
  • First Born (siblings)

Dystopian

  • Best Friend (addicted to a virtual friends product, themes of loneliness & addiction) ***only 5 minutes but 10/10 impact
  • Less Than Human (zombies but it’s cute)

Funny

  • Yes-People (different meanings of “yes”)
  • Alarm
  • All You Can Eat

Misc

  • The Twins
  • Destiny
